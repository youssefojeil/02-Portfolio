About Me

Hello! My name is Youssef Ojeil, I graduated from UCF in 2016 with a degree in Electrical Engineering. I worked as an Electrical Engineer for 4.5 years, 2.5 years as a test engineer at Micross Components and 2 years as a product engineer at Honeywell. I took a break from my engineering career to find my passion. Although my expertise was in hardware, I had to learn & use programming languages such as python, C & C++. I grew up as a gamer, I love sports & anime. In the past year and a half I immersed myself in blockchain technology & web3. As I continued to research I started to fall in love with the technology behind it & for the remainder of 2021 & 2022 I worked in the field. During that time I was working with a lot of developers & designers and had to self learn to keep up. After years of using code through self taught methods & working with developers, I decided to enroll in a full stack bootcamp to learn the fundamentals with a solid foundation.
